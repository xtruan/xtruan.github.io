MultiPong for Android

 

MultiPong is an Android implementation of the classic Pong game between two players over Bluetooth. The game is played with two Android devices. The players use an in-app wizard to connect their devices using the Bluetooth protocol. After the devices are connected, the game can be started. Players tilt their device left and right to make their paddle move back and forth on the screen to prevent the puck from hitting the wall. When the puck reaches the opposite side of their device, it jumps to the other player's device who then must defend their wall. Special attention had to be paid to synchronization between devices. Information about which device controls the puck, the position of both players paddles, and each user's score is transmitted over the Bluetooth link. An additional feature is the maintaining of a high score list which is stored in a file on each device.
